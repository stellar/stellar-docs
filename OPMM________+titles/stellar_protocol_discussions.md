## June 4 2020 – Open protocol discussion (first broadcast)

- **Title & Date**: *Open protocol discussion* – June 4 2020 (6:00 PM UTC) 
- **Summary**: Stellar’s first broadcast of the bi‑weekly **Open Protocol Discussion** focused on Core Advancement Proposals for Protocol 14.  The post explains that the meeting is a technical discussion and recommends that viewers read CAP‑33 (New Sponsored Reserves), CAP‑23 (Two‑part Payments) and issues 622 and 624 for context.  It notes that these items are hosted in the public GitHub repository and invites community members to participate via the Stellar developers Google Group【667968709545790†L31-L50】.

## January 28 2021 – Open protocol discussion

- **Title & Date**: *Open protocol discussion* – January 28 2021 (7:00 PM UTC)
- **Summary**: This meeting focused on a Core Advancement Proposal that would allow an issuer to **claw back an asset** to meet regulatory requirements.  The post clarifies that the new asset flag and operations would not affect existing assets and would be transparent to asset holders【616413476322728†L24-L33】.
